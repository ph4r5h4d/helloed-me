Jokes aside, receiving a one-word greeting can be confusing and unproductive.

But we can fix that together.

Why This Matters:

Why This Matters:

  • Saves Time - Get to the point quicker.
  • Builds Rapport - Show genuine interest.
  • Improves Communication - Clearer, more meaningful interactions.

Do:

  • Start with a purpose: "Hi, can we discuss the project?"
  • Add context: "Hello, I have a quick question about..."
  • Show interest: "Hey, how was your weekend?"

Don't:

  • Just say "Hi" and leave it hanging.
  • Use one-word greetings.
  • Be vague or unclear.

Examples of Effective Greetings:

  • "Hi Farshad, can we chat about the marketing strategy?"
  • "Hello Farshad, I wanted to ask you about the meeting notes."
  • "Hej Farsjad, hope you had a great weekend! Quick question about our project..."
